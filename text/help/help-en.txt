/start - I welcome you!
/cancel - Cancels the current operation.
/lang language - Changes the language (es = Spanish - en = English)
/homework - Stores homework.
/delete - Deletes homework.
/show - Shows all the homework you currently have!
/rn number1 number2 - Gives you a random number between number1 and number2
/h - Stores homework in just one message. Command: /h [Subject] - [Homework] - [Date (Optional)]
/m text - Sends a message to a group anonimously
/configm - Tells me where do I send stuff (with /m, photos, videos, etc). Just put this command in the group you want me to send them.
